The Challenge

Why Tennis Still Feels
Disconnected

The tennis world is fragmented across dozens of tools, platforms, and manual processes. We're here to change that.

Finding Players Is Difficult

There is no reliable way to discover opponents of your level nearby. Players rely on word of mouth, message boards, and luck.

Clubs Operate in Isolation

Each club manages bookings, memberships, and schedules on separate systems with no shared visibility across the sport.

Tournament Information Is Fragmented

Entry deadlines, draws, results, and rankings are scattered across multiple governing body websites, spreadsheets, and PDFs.

Coaches Struggle to Reach Players

Finding new students, managing schedules, and tracking player progress still happens through WhatsApp groups and paper notebooks.

No Unified Tennis Identity

Players carry no portable digital profile. Every club, tournament, and programme requires the same information to be entered again from scratch.

Communities Are Spread Thin

Tennis conversations happen across Facebook Groups, WhatsApp, Reddit, Discord, and club notice boards — never in one focused place.

Our Answer

One Platform.
Every Tennis Connection.

10isConnect is the digital home for the entire tennis ecosystem — designed from the ground up to eliminate fragmentation and bring every part of the sport together.

Smart Player Discovery

Centralised Tournament Hub

Portable Digital Profile

One verified identity — LTA number, WTN, achievements, and history — follows you everywhere.

Coach Marketplace
